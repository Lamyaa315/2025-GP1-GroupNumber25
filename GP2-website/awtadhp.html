What AWTAD offers

A clearer way to understand muscle activation balance.

AWTAD combines EMG session recording, analysis results, and coach feedback in one simple platform. It is built to support athlete awareness and training decisions, not to diagnose medical conditions.

EMG Signal Preview

After a session, users can view a downsampled signal preview that gives a clear visual glimpse of the recorded muscle activity.

Symmetry Index

The system summarizes left-right muscle activation balance using a symmetry index that is easy to read and compare.

Dominant Side Detection

AWTAD highlights which side showed stronger activation during the recorded exercise to support better movement awareness.

Coach-Client Connection

Athletes can connect with a coach, allowing the coach to view sessions, review performance indicators, and provide feedback.

Session Feedback

Coaches can write feedback on a selected session, and the client can see it later inside their session history.

Simple Dashboards

Clean dashboards help clients and coaches view sessions, results, and training records without complex clinical interfaces.

How it works

From sensor placement to actionable feedback.

Connect Sensors

The user follows the placement guide and connects the EMG sensors before starting the movement session.

Record Exercise

The athlete performs the selected exercise while the system records muscle activation from both sides.

Analyze Balance

AWTAD processes the session and presents key outputs such as signal preview, symmetry index, and dominant side.

Review Feedback

Connected coaches can review session details and send comments that appear for the client.
